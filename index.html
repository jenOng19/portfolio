About Me

Hello, I’m Jennifer Ong and I am passionate about coding, development, and learning about how technology can assist in improving common key business processes.

Throughout my years in Cal State Fullerton, I developed a passion for starting my own businesses where I created my own websites to showcase products that I sell online. This led me to a career in marketing where I had the opportunity to create and maintain a website for the Consumer Electronics Show that happens every year in Las Vegas. Attending the CES shows opened my eyes to how powerful technology is and it’s rapid growth through the years.

After two years of starting my own startups and seeing how technology can improve businesses, I am now in web development. My goal is to incorporate my love for technology and business while developing projects utilizing AI.
